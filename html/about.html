Don Dukes, President
Past president of El Paso International Chapter American Concrete Institute, Don Dukes has been serving the City of El Paso for 26 years. Training certifications from Sika Chemical "concrete repair", Dayton Superior Institute of Concrete Technology for Chemical and Cement products, Lavina "Concrete Polishing". Involved with and supplying engineering for tilt wall lifting and bracing supplies. Over 20 years experience in selling concrete forming, shoring, scaffolding, and concrete accessories.
I am grateful to have had the opportunity to first serve concrete construction as a material and equipment supplier. This not only gave me access to contractors building with concrete, but also access to chemists and engineers in the industry as problem solvers and manufacturers for production of quality materials and equipment. I served and learned from manufacturers.
Each of the services that I perform, I have been given the knowledge of products and equipment during class, with hands on training, from those that develop and manufacture performance products. I have worked with contractors, architects, engineers, and chemists, sharing information allowing craftsmen to give owners what they expect from their concrete foundation and structure.
We are commited to quality craft.
SCSI is a subcontractor to concrete contractors who need professional experience in concrete repairs, architectural concrete finishes, and tilt wall concrete finishes to panel surface and repairs to reveals and chamfers.
